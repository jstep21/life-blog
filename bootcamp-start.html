The Decision to Dive In: Preparing for Tech Elevator

After months of research into software engineering bootcamps, I've finally decided to take my web developing skills to the next stage by joining the Tech Elevator bootcamp!

March 11, 2024 Jake Stephens

In the months leading up to my enrollment in Tech Elevator's National Live Remote bootcamp, I was on a mission to find the right program to expand my coding knowledge and advance my career. The decision wasn't one I took lightly; it involved extensive research, countless hours of reading reviews, comparing curricula, and speaking with alumni. I also underwent rigorous assessments and interviews to ensure that the bootcamp I chose would be the perfect fit for my learning style and career goals. After much consideration, Tech Elevator stood out as the ideal platform to propel me forward in the world of software development.

What drew me to Tech Elevator was its comprehensive curriculum and strong focus on full-stack development. The prospect of diving deep into Java, a language known for its versatility and robustness, was particularly exciting. I was also eager to gain hands-on experience with Spring Boot, a powerful framework for building enterprise-level applications. But it wasn't just the back-end technologies that piqued my interest; the chance to learn Vue.js for front-end development promised to round out my skill set, enabling me to create dynamic, user-friendly web applications. Additionally, the program's emphasis on Agile methodology was a major draw, as it would equip me with the skills needed to work effectively in modern, collaborative development environments.

As I prepared to embark on this 14-week journey, my excitement was tinged with a healthy dose of anticipation. I knew that the road ahead would be challenging, filled with long nights of coding, problem-solving, and continuous learning. But I was also confident that the support from Tech Elevator’s experienced instructors and the structured, immersive nature of the bootcamp would provide me with the foundation I needed to thrive. This was more than just a course—it was the beginning of a transformative journey that would set the stage for my future as a software developer.
